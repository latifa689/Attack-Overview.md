## Attack Overview

A cybersecurity attack is a malicious attempt to gain unauthorized access
To systems, networks, or data with the intent to steal, disrupt, or damage
Information assets.

This project analyzes a network-based cyber attack that exploits system
Vulnerabilities to compromise organizational infrastructure.

### Attack Type
- Network-based attack
- Possible exploitation of unpatched systems

### Target
- Enterprise network infrastructure
- Critical services and data
